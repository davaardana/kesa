Complete Fiber Optic Services

From installation to maintenance — we handle every aspect of your fiber optic network

Connection & Termination

High-precision fiber optic cable splicing and termination utilising state-of-the-art splicers and OTDR measuring instruments to ensure optimal network quality.

FTTH Work

Fiber to the Home — Direct fiber optic network installation to residential and commercial premises, encompassing cable pulling, splicing, and termination through to ONT activation.

Get a Quote

Horizontal Directional Drilling

Underground excavation method for duct and fiber optic cable installation without disturbing road surfaces. An efficient solution for urban and densely populated areas.

Get a Quote

Network Construction

End-to-end fiber optic cable network construction from planning, surveying, and installation through to commissioning. Covering backbone, distribution, and access networks.

Material Supply
